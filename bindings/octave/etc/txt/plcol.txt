  plcol(color)
  ************

  plcol

  Sets the color for subsequent lines.

  color (PLINT, input): Integer representing the color. The defaults at
  present are (these may change):

  1. black (default background)
  2. red
  3. yellow
  4. green
  5. aquamarine
  6. pink
  7. wheat
  8. grey
  9. brown
 10. blue
 11. BlueViolet
 12. cyan
 13. turquoise
 14. magenta
 15. salmon
 16. white (default foreground)
