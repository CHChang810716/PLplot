    Documentation

    		PLplot comes with a comprehensive (but out of date) manual. You can download the manual in any of the following formats:
    • Postscript
    • DVI
    • GNU info
    Or you can view it online.

    Mailing List
    There is a PLplot mailing list for users and developers. You can subscribe to the mailing list by sending an email to plplot-general-request@plplot.org, containing the message: 
    	subscribe
    You can also view an archive of the mailing list.
